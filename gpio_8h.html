Enumerator:

The GPIO pin acts as an input and is pulled down.

GPIO_INPUT_PULLUP 

The GPIO pin acts as an input and is pulled up.

GPIO_OUTPUT 

The GPIO pin acts as an output.

GPIO_UNUSED 

The GPIO pin is not used Unused pins are configured as outputs and connected to GND
