enum led

Enumeration for the on-board LEDs.

This enumeration is used to select the LED(s) to switch on/off in the service procedures.

Enumerator:
LED_ALL 

Select all LEDs.

enum led_mode

Enumeration for the LED mode.

Depending on the mode, an LED can be turned on/off automatically or manually.

Enumerator:
LED_STATIC 

The LED can be turned on/off manually.

LED_FLASH 

The LED flashes briefly and remains off.

LED_BLINK 

The LED blinks once per second.
